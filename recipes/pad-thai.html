Ingredients

Serves 2
  • 400g vermincelli rice noodles
  • 1 lime
  • 4 tbsp peanut butter
  • 2 tbsp tamarind paste
  • 5 tbsp soy sauce
  • 400g tofu
  • rapeseed oil
    •

    Process:

    1. Put the noodles in a pan of boiling water and leave to cook for a few minutes. Once done remove from the pan and drop into a bowl with some oil to stop them sticking.
    2. Stick the tofu into the pan with some oil and cook each side until golden brown. Remove from the pan and set aside.
    3. Mix the rest of the ingredients in a bowl to taste.
    4. Toss noods into pan on medium heat and cover with the sauce.
    5. Mix in the tofu and enjoy.
