Pasta recipe

What is pasta?

Are you crazy? You don't know what pasta is?!

I'll assume you're kidding and we'll proceed.

Ingredients

  • Packet of pasta
  • Water
  • Salt

Process

  1. Fill a pan with water and bring to a boil
  2. Heavily salt the water. Allow it to all dissolve. Try get it to taste as salty as the sea.
  3. Take the pasta out of the packet - careful not to break any here - and place into the boiling water.
  4. Let it cook for a few minutes, checking to see if it is al dente.
  5. Once done, add to any pasta sauce of your choice.
