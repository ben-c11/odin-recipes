Beans on Toast recipe

What is beans on toast?

Beans on toast is life. Enough said. I'll show you how to make some banging B on T.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Butter
  • BRANSTONS beans
  • optional cheddar cheese
  • optional bbq sauce

Process

  1. Put beans in pan & heat up gently. Give them a stir when bubbling and occasionally whilst heating to prevent sticking to pan. You can also use the microwave if you have one.
  2. Meanwhile, chop a few slices of fresh sourdough loaf and pop in the toaster - or toast in the pan.
  3. Once beans are done, season to taste. If desired cheese and let it melt and/or add bbq sauce.
  4. Butter the bread shortly after it is toasted to melt butter.
  5. Serve and enjoy.
